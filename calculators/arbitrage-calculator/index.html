Arbitrage Betting Calculator

Enter decimal odds and total stake to check whether the combined book percentage is below 100%.

Formula

Book percentage = 1/odds A + 1/odds B + optional 1/odds C. If the percentage is below 100%, the difference is the theoretical arbitrage margin before errors, voids, limits, fees and odds movement.
